Privacy Policy – Amazon Product Inspector

Amazon Product Inspector does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information.

The extension processes product information that is visible on Amazon product pages solely to provide inspection, review insights, listing checks, and comparison features. All data processing occurs locally within the user’s browser.

Saved products and preferences are stored locally using Chrome’s storage API and are not transmitted to any external servers.

The extension does not use analytics, tracking, advertising, or third-party services. No user data is sold or shared.

This extension is not affiliated with or endorsed by Amazon.

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you may contact the developer through the Chrome Web Store listing.
